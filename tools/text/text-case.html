    Text Case Converter

    Convert text between common naming and writing styles used in code, URLs, and prose

    What gets converted

    • camelCase, PascalCase, snake_case, and kebab-case are common identifier styles in codebases and URLs.
    • Sentence case and Title Case are useful for user-facing copy and documentation.
    • Input is tokenized by whitespace, punctuation, dashes, underscores, and camelCase boundaries before each output is rebuilt in the target format.
