    RankWordCountShare

    Counting details

    • Text is normalized to lowercase, punctuation is stripped, and apostrophes inside words are preserved.
    • When stop-word filtering is enabled, common filler words are excluded so technical terms stand out.
    • Share is calculated as count divided by total matched words after filtering.
