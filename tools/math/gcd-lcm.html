    GCD / LCM Calculator

    Find the greatest common divisor and least common multiple for any list of integers

    Math background

    • GCD is the largest positive integer that divides every input without a remainder.
    • LCM is the smallest positive integer that every input divides evenly into.
    • The calculator uses Euclid's algorithm, which repeatedly replaces a pair (a, b) with (b, a mod b) until the remainder is zero.
